Assembly Guide (actually)

Step 1: Print your Heather

  • Layer height: 0.2 mm
  • Supports: Build plate only
  • Infill: 15%
  • Colour changes: Up to 4 (for maximum fashion)

Step 2: Insert the LED

  • Insert the LED into the back of Heather: anode (long leg) on the right, cathode (short leg) on the left

Step 3: Add the resistor

  • Twist the resistor leg to connect to the LED anode
  • Clip the extra wire after twisting

Step 4: Bend into position

  • Bend the leads at right angles so they aim toward the header pins
